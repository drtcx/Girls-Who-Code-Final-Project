Take Action

Making environments safe for undocumented students

  • Use terms like undocumented and immigrant. Be aware that some undocumented students like the term Dreamers, but not all. Ask to make sure, though.
  • Speak inclusively. When talking about college applications, say, Today, we are going to talk about applying for the FAFSA, state financial aid, and other loans. Or say, Here are some scholarships that require a social security number, and here are some scholarships that do not. Present options in an unprompted, undisturbed way that allow undocumented students to be included in the conversation.
